Every business needs to do accounting to know about the results of the business and to manage cash flow. Organizations hire accountants for this purpose, train, and manage them through the course of their employment. Bookkeeping is the first step in accounting and financial reporting.

A bookkeeper posts day-to-day transactions in books of ledgers or accounting softwares. He does not prepare financial statements but posting of transactions affects all G/L accounts and that is how trail balance and chart of accounts affect the changes in financial statements. Therefore, it is very important to have a bookkeeper that can post accounting transactions timely and accurately.

We understand the above problem many business owners face these days and we provide tailor made solutions after meeting with prospect clients and understanding their requirement. We provide full time or part time accounting staff on daily, weekly, and monthly or assignment basis as per your business need. These accountants will take care of accounting, bookkeeping, financial reporting, and cash management. This reduces your headache of hiring and managing accounting staff.

Axon accounting services provide you bookkeeping and accounting solutions that take all the stress away from your business. Our bookkeepers are available to you and work for your business. We take over all your stress of hiring and managing bookkeeping staff and ensure accuracy and reliability. We handle all your accounting and financial matters and you can focus on your business operations.

Our senior consultants manage our bookkeeping staff and take report from them on weekly basis to make sure that the best services are provided to you. We provide off site and on site accounting services, i.e. you can send your supporting documents through mail/courier and our staff will maintain your books of accounts within our office premises OR a part time accountant can be assigned to visit your office to carry out the job of a bookkeeper.
